FAQ

  • What are parallel tracks?

    We're holding parallel tracks to cater to a wider audience based on the interest. While the GDG track is going to deal with Machine Learning, Computer Vision and TensorFlow, the WTM track will deal with basics of Machine Learning and Firebase followed by a design sprint hosted by a Googler!

  • Who can attend?

    The event is open for everyone. Depending on your interest, you can attend a particular track.

  • What should I bring?

    All the participants are required to bring their laptops and any material that the team requires to use during the hackathon.

  • Will there be provision for food?

    Yes, lunch that includes pizzas from dominos will be provided free of cost.
